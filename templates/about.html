ABOUT GOPAL MANDIR 🔖

Gopal Mandir situated at Purana Chowk, Shahganj in District Jaunpur, which was established many decades ago by group people of jaiswal society which is a temple of Lord Shree Krishna, Laxmi Narayan, Ram Darbar, Lord Shiva, an abode of God, a Hindu house of worship, and a spiritual and cultural home dedicated to devotion, learning and harmony. Timeless Hindu spiritual messages, vibrant devotional traditions and ancient architecture all are echoed in its art and architecture. The mandir is a humble tribute to Bhagwan Shri Narayan, Shri Ram, Krishna, Lord Shiva and Devi Laxmi, Gauri, Durga, Sita, the avatars, devas and great sages of Hinduism. The traditionally-styled trust complex was renovated and expanded many times depend upon needs and requirements with the blessings of Hindus and through the devoted efforts of skilled artisans and volunteers. Which is governed by registered trust with it’s rule.

OUR PROGRAMS

• To maintain, manage, renovate and expand all the programs of the temple • To do work for development of Character and mental of peoples of society of Hinduism • To arrange and manage of special program (Parve) for the beneficiary of Society • To arrange and manage of group marriage (Samuhik Vivah) for the weaker’s section of Hindu Society • To do all activity and management for the development of all the peoples of society including playing important role for the establishment this temple also for the temple and trust • To arrange and manage of educational program, to established education institution for the beneficiary of Society including Hindu culture • To arrange and manage of training program for establishment of employment • To arrange and promote religious program • To do all activity for the development of Country and Society • To do work for the development of weaker’s section of society • To provide the facilitation for the helpless and sick person of society • To provide the facilitation for the development of weaker’s section of society including children and older person • To do all other works which is passed by trust board time to time
